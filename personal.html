My Name is Adam Puckeridge, I was born in Australia & grew up in the Sutherland Shire in NSW.

I started playing guitar at the age of 13 and also took up drums around the age of 15. I have played drums in multiple bands in Sydney.

I left school in year 11 to take a job teaching guitar and drums with the hope of being able to be a professional musician. After many years of pursuing that dream I realised it was something I was not 100% committed to and would struggle to make it as a professional musician if I was not committed fully.

In 2017 I moved to the NSW Central West with my partner and our 2 dogs & soon after took on a stray as a third. Around the same time I started to learn Japanese as a second language (with English as my first) & would love to visit Japan.

I also enjoy reading and my favourite author is Stephen King.
